About SSOA

Kris and her partners, Dr. Wade Faerber and Dr. Bret Powers, opened Surgical Specialists of Alaska in Anchorage on March 2, 2015. A second clinic in Wasilla was opened in January 2017.

SSOA offers elite Orthopedic and Primary Care. In addition to Orthopedic Surgery, we will extend our scope to General Surgery in the near future! Visit us in Anchorage or our new Wasilla location!

Meet The Team

Dr. Wade Faerber, D.O.

Sports Medicine

Wade Faerber DO, FAOAO is a fellowship trained sports medicine board certified orthopedic surgeon based in Riverside, California. He has been in practice since 1992. Dr. Faerber graduated from S.U.N.Y at Oneonta in 1982 with a BS majoring in Biology.

Dr. Bret Powers, D.O.

Sports Medicine

Dr. Powers graduated with his Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine degree from the Western University of Health Sciences in Pomona, California. After a residency from the Summa Health Care System he went on to attain his fellowship form Ohio State University Sports Medicine Center. Dr. Powers specializes in the field of Sports Medicine.

Dr. Nathan Ehmer, D.O.

Trauma Surgery

Dr. Nathan Ehmer is a board certified and fellowship trained orthopedic surgeon specializing in orthopaedic trauma, complex fracture care, pelvic & acetabular reconstruction and total joint replacement. He has specialized training in anterior hip replacement as well as Mako Robotic assisted and patient specific hip and knee replacement surgery. In addition, Dr. Ehmer enjoys a robust general orthopedic practice.

Kris Kile, R.N. MSN, FNP-C

Sports Medicine

With a collective 17 years in Orthopedic and Primary Care, Kris Kile provides extensive knowledge and experience. Kris strives to assist patients in maintaining their general and orthopedic health; from common cold to acute injury.
