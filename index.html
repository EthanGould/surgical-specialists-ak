About SSOA

Providers Kris Kile ANP-C, Dr. Wade Faerber and Dr. Bret Powers, opened Surgical Specialists of Alaska in Anchorage with a fresh perspective in treating with a "patients' first attitude" in March 2015. With their success a second clinic in Wasilla was opened in January 2017.

Our excellent staff members and highly-experienced providers at SSOA have over 30 years of combined medical experience offering quality care for our patients! We specialize in Sports Medicine & Orthopedic Surgery, Trauma, Joint replacement, General Surgery.

We are commited to educating our patients with the necessary information to obtain optimal health; including surgery options, side effects and post-operative support. We respect each of our patient's rights to privacy and rights to choose their plan of care.

Thank you for choosing SSOA!

Appointments

Welcome to SSOA! Thank you for choosing us for your health care needs. The following information is intended to provide a summary of our practice's policies and procedures. For any questions or concerns you may contact our office staff from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday through Friday at 907 . 277 . 9700 [option 1] for our Anchorage clinic; or from 9:00 AM to 4:30 pm Monday thru Thursday at 907 . 277 . 9700 [option 2] for our Wasilla clinic.

Patients

  • New patients should arrive 30 minutes prior to your first visit to complete the necessary paperwork before your scheduled appointment time.
  • Established patients should arrive 5 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment time to allow for any updates, if necessary.
  • If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may be rescheduled.
  • If you are unable to keep your appointment, please call the office as soon as possible to reschedule.

Prescription Refills

  • If you require a refill of a prescription, please contact your pharmacy directly, so that your refill request can be submitted to our office electronically.
  • If you have run out of refills, please call our office at (907)277-9700 to leave the following information on our voicemail system: your full name, date of birth, name of the medicine(s), the prescription number on the bottle, your phone number and the name, address and phone number of the pharmacy.
  • Please refrain from leaving multiple messages regarding your prescriptions; our Medical Assistants will call you back as soon as possible.

Emergencies

  • If you are experiencing an emergency call 911 or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

Payment Options

  • All co-payments, co-insurances and applied deductibles are to be paid at the time of service.
  • Payment in full is required at the time service is rendered for all private-pay patients; unless otherwise arranged with management.
  • We accept cash, checks, Master Card, and VISA as payment for services rendered.

Policies

Late Patients

  • We are committed to pormpt service, and will work very hard, apart from emergencies, to stay on time. You may be asked to reschedule your appointment if you arrive 15 minutes or more after your scheduled appointment time.
  • Established patients arrive 10 to 15 minutes early to your appointment to complete paperwork.
  • New patients please arrive 30 to 45 minutes early to your appointment to complete paperwork.

Prescriptions

  • Calls for prescriptions recieved after 3:00pm will be addressed the following day.
  • Please try to call the office at least 48 hours in advance for all medications.
  • Calls recieved after 12:30pm on Friday will be returned on the following Monday.
  • Always leave your full name, date of birth, name of medicine(s) [spelling it out if necessary], the prescription number on the bottle, your phone number and the name, phone number and address of the Pharmacy.

Medical Records

  • To obtain a copy of your records for personal reasons, or to give to another practice please fill out a Release of Information (ROI) and return it to the front desk receptionist.
  • We do charge for copies of Medical Records and prices can be provided to you at the front desk
  • There is no charge for records being requested if you have been referred to our clinic, or if we are referring you out.
  • Please note that we give up to 30 business days to complete it as soon as possible.
  • All urgent record requests will take priority over all other records.
  • The Notice of Privacy Practiced is available at the front desk, or you can click here for a copy.
